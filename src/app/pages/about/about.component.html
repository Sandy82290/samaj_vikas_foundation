Our Story

Building a Sustainable & Empowered Society

Founded in 2015, Samaj Vikas Foundation (समाज विकास फाउंडेशन) began as a small group of volunteers determined to make a difference in the lives of the underprivileged. What started as a single learning centre has grown into a multi-programme organisation serving thousands of people across more than ten villages.

We believe that real, lasting change happens when communities are empowered to lead their own development. Through education, healthcare, women empowerment and environmental initiatives, we work hand-in-hand with the people we serve — not for them, but with them.

Our Vision

“Creating a sustainable and empowered society” — where every individual, regardless of background, has the opportunity to live with dignity, access education and healthcare, and contribute to a thriving community.

Our Mission

To support underprivileged communities through education, health and social development — delivering practical, transparent and sustainable programmes that create measurable, long-term impact.
